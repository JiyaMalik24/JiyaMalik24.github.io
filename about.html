I am an 18 year old self taught painter from New Delhi.

I have been drawing and painting for several years now, it is how i express myself - my emotions, imagination, and reality. This authentic expression of my creativity has helped me understand how I perceive life itself and what new I make of it, immensely supporting me in discovery of my individuality. In such process of making something uniquely mine, I experience a feeling of peace that I never really sense doing anything else. I am so peacefullyimmersed in painting, because I have a self-driven goal in mind for my art, that has nothing to do with how much time it takes me, or how objectively good others find it to be – it is unabashedly and truly about my own self, my emotions, and my ambition. It feels like a competition with my past being and not with the world, as it usually is, because when I am painting, I am determined to excel at expressing my art on my terms, and no one else’s.
